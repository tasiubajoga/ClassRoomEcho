The Problem

The silent crisis in Nigerian classrooms.

Every school day, thousands of students fall behind — not because they are unwilling to learn, but because they simply weren't there to hear the instruction the first time.

🏫

Absenteeism Rate
Nearly half of all secondary school students in Nigeria miss at least one lesson per month due to illness, family obligations, transport failures or late arrival.
📞

Information Distortion

How It Works

Three steps. Zero complexity.

ClassroomEcho is designed to be picked up by any teacher in under 60 seconds — no technical training required.

1
Teacher Records
After class, the teacher opens ClassroomEcho's Teacher Studio, selects a subject, titles the instruction, and presses Record. The instruction is captured in full — in the teacher's own voice.
Takes As little as 60 seconds to set up and start recording. Works on any browser — Chrome, Firefox, Edge. No app download needed.
2
System Stores It
The recording is saved instantly, labelled with the subject name, teacher's name and date. Students can see a live count of new recordings waiting for them in the portal.
Organised by Subject, date, teacher name and class. Students can filter, search, and sort with one tap.
3
Student Listens
Students log in with their name, class, and admission number. They browse recordings by subject, press play, control speed, seek to any point, and mark recordings as listened.
Student controls 0.75× to 2× speed, 10s rewind/skip, seek bar, listen history — built for focused revision.

Features

Everything a classroom needs. Nothing it doesn't.

ClassroomEcho is purpose-built for Nigerian secondary schools — not adapted from a generic product. Every feature addresses a real classroom pain point.

🎙
One-Tap Recording
Teachers record instructions in seconds. Live waveform visualiser shows the mic is working. Automatic chunked capture ensures no audio is lost even on slow connections.
🏷️
Subject-Tagged Library
Every recording is tagged with subject, teacher and date. Students see a colour-coded subject grid and can filter to exactly the class they need in one tap.
⚡
Variable Speed Playback
Students control their own pace — 0.75× for careful comprehension or 2× to quickly review a familiar topic. Speed setting persists per recording session.
✓
Listen History
Students mark recordings as "Listened". New recordings are badged. The dashboard tracks total listened vs total available — building accountability and study habits.
🔍
Search & Sort
Search by title, subject or teacher name. Sort by newest, oldest, unlistened, or A–Z. Finding a specific instruction takes under 5 seconds, even in large recording libraries.
📥
Download for Offline
Teachers can download any recording as an audio file. Students without reliable internet can save recordings to their device and listen offline at home or during study hall.
🔒
Student Authentication
Students log in with their name, class arm and admission number. Only enrolled students in the right class see the recordings intended for them — no public access.
🌊
Live Waveform Display
Both the Teacher Studio and the Student Player show animated red waveforms — giving real-time visual feedback during recording and playback to confirm audio is working.
📱
Works on Any Device
Fully responsive — teachers can record from a laptop in the staffroom; students can listen on a smartphone during break. No special hardware or operating system required.
